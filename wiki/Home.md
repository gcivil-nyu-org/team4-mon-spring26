# TenantGuard NYC - Wiki

Welcome to the **TenantGuard NYC** wiki -- the central knowledge base for our ML-powered platform that helps NYC tenants make informed housing decisions.

## About

TenantGuard NYC aggregates public housing data from NYC Open Data (HPD violations, 311 complaints, building registrations) and uses machine learning to generate building-level risk scores. The platform provides an interactive city heatmap, detailed building profiles, landlord portfolio views, community discussion forums, and risk-change alert subscriptions -- empowering prospective and current tenants to evaluate safety before signing a lease.
